Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I book a Service Provider ?
You can browse available Service Providers, filter them based on ratings, prices, and services, and submit a booking request with the required details.
2. How does the payment system work ?
To confirm a booking, you need to pay 10% of the total amount to Home&Heart. The remaining 90% is paid in cash directly to the Service Provider after service completion.
3. Can I cancel my booking and get a refund ?
Yes, you can cancel within 10 minutes of payment for a full refund. After this period, refunds will not be issued. Refunds are processed within 24 hours.
4. How do I find the right Service Provider for my needs ?
You can search, filter, and sort Service Providers based on service type, price, ratings, and availability to find the most suitable provider.
5. Can I communicate with my Service Provider before the service?
Yes, the platform includes an in-built calling feature for direct communication.
